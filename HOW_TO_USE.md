## 2. Manage Your Parking Spots

Access the **Listed Parking** section to manage spots you own or manage.

**Create a Listing**
*   Tap the **Plus (+)** icon to add a new parking location (Listing).
*   Enter details like Name, Location, Total Spots, and Hourly Rates.

**Manage Status**
*   **My Listings:** View your listings under the **"My"** tab.
*   **Edit:** Tap the **Pencil** icon to update listing details (e.g., change rates or add floors).
*   **Delete:** Tap the **Trash** icon to remove a listing.

**Spot Control**
*   Tap on a listing to see individual spots.
*   Manually mark spots as **Open**, **Occupied**, or **Closed** to keep availability accurate for other users.
